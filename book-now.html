Before You Reserve

📍 Setup Area

Please ensure a reasonably level installation area is available and clear of rocks, branches, pet waste, toys, and other obstacles before delivery.

⚡ Power Requirements

A dedicated 120V household electrical outlet must be available within reach of the blower location. Extension cords are supplied when required.

👨‍👩‍👧 Adult Supervision

A responsible adult must supervise participants at all times while the inflatable is in operation.
