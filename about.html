👋 Our Story

Hi! We're Kris and Jordynne Denty, the owners of Bounce Boss Inflatables. Jordynne was born and raised in Fort Saskatchewan, and today we're proud to be raising our own family in the same community we call home. We started Bounce Boss to build something that brings families together while providing clean, safe, and reliable inflatable rentals backed by the kind of service we'd proudly offer our own friends and neighbours. We're excited to continue growing our business, expanding our inflatable lineup, and helping create unforgettable memories throughout Northeast Alberta.

💜 Our Promise

Every rental is treated as if it were for our own family. From your first message until the inflatable is packed away, our goal is to make your experience simple, professional, and stress-free.

🧼 Clean Every Time

Every inflatable is thoroughly cleaned, sanitized, and carefully inspected before every rental so every family can enjoy a clean, safe, and worry-free experience.

🛡️ Safety Is Our Priority

Our commercial inflatables are operated in accordance with applicable Alberta amusement ride requirements, AEDARSA guidance, manufacturer instructions, and recognized ASTM safety standards.

🤝 Honest Service

We believe in clear communication, fair pricing, and showing up when we say we will. Our reputation is built one event at a time.

🌟 Why We Love What We Do

Bounce Boss is more than just a business to us—it's something we're building for our family and our community. Our family has deep roots in Fort Saskatchewan, and we're proud to continue raising our family here while serving the community we call home. There's nothing better than seeing kids laughing, parents relaxing, and families making memories together. Knowing Bounce Boss gets to be a small part of those special moments is exactly why we started this business. Whether it's a backyard birthday, school fun day, sports wind-up, or community celebration, we're honoured every time someone trusts us to be part of their event.

🚀 Looking Ahead

Bounce Boss is just getting started. As we continue to grow, our goal is to expand our fleet of commercial inflatables, add exciting new attractions, and continue investing in high-quality equipment so we can serve even more families throughout Northeast Alberta. No matter how much we grow, one thing will never change—our commitment to providing friendly service, clean equipment, and a rental experience you can count on.

🧁 Supporting Local
