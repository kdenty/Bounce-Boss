About Bounce Boss

Family Owned. Community Focused.

Bounce Boss Inflatables is proud to be a locally owned, family-operated business based in Fort Saskatchewan, providing clean, safe, commercial-quality inflatable rentals throughout Northeast Alberta.

Family Owned Commercial Grade Safety Focused Professional Service Proudly Local

Our Story

Hi! We're Kris and Jordynne Denty, the owners of Bounce Boss Inflatables. Jordynne was born and raised in Fort Saskatchewan, and today we're raising our own family in the same community we proudly call home. We started Bounce Boss with one simple goal: To provide clean, safe, commercial-quality inflatable rentals backed by honest service, clear communication, and equipment we'd feel completely comfortable setting up for our own children. Whether you're planning a birthday party, school event, community celebration, sports wind-up, or corporate gathering, we want the rental process to be simple, stress-free, and one of the highlights of your event. And if building this business helps fund a family vacation every now and then… well… we certainly won't complain.

Our Mission

Clean & Sanitized

Every inflatable is thoroughly cleaned, sanitized, and inspected before every rental so families can enjoy their event with confidence.

Safety First

Professional setup, secure anchoring, and careful inspections are part of every rental because safety always comes first.

Friendly Service

We believe great customer service is just as important as great equipment. From your first message until pickup, we're here to help.

Our Promise

Commercial-Grade Equipment Professional Setup Secure Installation Clean & Sanitized Friendly Communication Reliable Service

Every rental receives the same attention we'd expect for our own family. Our goal is simple: deliver dependable service, quality equipment, and help create unforgettable memories for every customer.

Looking Ahead

Bounce Boss is only getting started. As our business grows, we'll continue expanding our inflatable lineup, investing in new equipment, and serving even more families, schools, businesses, and community organizations throughout Northeast Alberta. We're excited for what's ahead and grateful that you're part of our journey.
