About Bounce Boss

Family owned. Community focused. Dedicated to bringing safe, clean and unforgettable fun to families across Fort Saskatchewan and the surrounding area.

📖 Our Story

Hi! We're Kris & Jordynne Denty, the owners of Bounce Boss Inflatables. We've been together since 2008 and are proud to call Fort Saskatchewan home. We're raising our family here and wanted to build a business that lets us serve the community we love while creating something meaningful for our family's future.

Bounce Boss was created with one goal: provide premium inflatable rentals backed by honest service, dependable communication and equipment we'd feel confident setting up for our own children.

❤️ Our Mission

Every rental is professionally cleaned, carefully inspected and safely installed before your event. We believe booking an inflatable should be easy, reliable and stress-free from start to finish.

🧼 Sanitized Every Rental 🛡 Safety First 🚚 Professional Setup 🏡 Proudly Local

Thank you for supporting a local family business. Every booking helps us continue building something we're truly proud of.
