About Bounce Boss

Family owned. Community focused. Dedicated to bringing safe, clean and unforgettable fun to families across Fort Saskatchewan and the surrounding area.

📖 Our Story

Hi, we're Kris and Jordynne Denty, the owners of Bounce Boss Inflatables! We're a family-owned business proudly based in Fort Saskatchewan. Jordynne was born and raised here, and we're now raising our own family in the same community we love. We started Bounce Boss to provide clean, safe, premium inflatable rentals backed by reliable service and great communication. We believe renting a bounce house should be easy, stress-free, and one of the highlights of your event. We're excited to grow our business, expand our inventory, and help create unforgettable memories for local families. And if it helps fund a family vacation or two along the way... well, we won't complain! 😉 Thank you for supporting a local family business. We look forward to helping make your next event unforgettable.

Bounce Boss was created with one goal: provide premium inflatable rentals backed by honest service, dependable communication and equipment we'd feel confident setting up for our own children.

❤️ Our Mission

Every rental is professionally cleaned, carefully inspected and safely installed before your event. We believe booking an inflatable should be easy, reliable and stress-free from start to finish.
