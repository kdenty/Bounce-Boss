❓ Frequently Asked Questions

💳 How do I reserve a rental?

Simply choose your preferred rental option, check availability, and contact us through our Book Now page. Your reservation is confirmed once Bounce Boss has approved your booking.

🌧️ What happens if it rains?

Safety always comes first. If severe weather makes setup unsafe, we'll work with you to reschedule whenever possible.

🏡 Do you deliver outside Fort Saskatchewan?

Absolutely. We proudly serve surrounding communities throughout Northeast Alberta. Additional travel fees may apply depending on your location.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Is adult supervision required?

Yes. A responsible adult must supervise the inflatable at all times to help ensure everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience.
