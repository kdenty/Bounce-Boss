❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of our most common questions. If you don't see what you're looking for, we're always happy to help.

💳 How do I reserve my rental?

Complete our booking request form and we'll contact you to confirm availability and finalize your reservation.

🌧️ What happens if it rains?

Safety comes first. If weather conditions prevent a safe setup, we'll work with you to reschedule whenever possible.

🏡 Do you travel outside Fort Saskatchewan?

Absolutely! We regularly deliver throughout Northeast Alberta. Contact us for a delivery quote if you're outside our standard service area.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Is adult supervision required?

Yes. A responsible adult must supervise the inflatable at all times to help ensure everyone has a fun and safe experience.
