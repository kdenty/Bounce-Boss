❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Still have questions? Here are answers to a few of the things we're asked most often.

💳 How do I reserve my rental?

Click any Book Now button to message us directly through Facebook Messenger. We'll confirm availability and answer any questions before finalizing your reservation.

🌧️ What happens if it rains?

Safety always comes first. If severe weather prevents a safe setup, we'll work with you to reschedule whenever possible.

🏡 Do you deliver outside Fort Saskatchewan?

Absolutely! We regularly provide inflatable rentals throughout Northeast Alberta. Contact us for a delivery quote if you're outside our standard service area.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Is adult supervision required?

Yes. A responsible adult must supervise the inflatable at all times while it is in use.
