What's Included With Every Rental

Professional Delivery

We deliver your inflatable directly to your event location, position it correctly, and complete a full setup before your guests arrive.

Safe Installation

Every inflatable is securely anchored using the appropriate method for the installation surface and inspected before use.

Complete Takedown

When your rental period is over, our team returns to safely deflate, pack, and remove the inflatable.

Rental Requirements

Level Setup Area Dedicated Power Source Adult Supervision Required Clear Access Required

To ensure a safe and successful setup, please provide a reasonably level installation area, clear access for delivery, and a dedicated electrical outlet within reach of the blower. If you have questions about your location, contact us before booking and we'll be happy to help.
